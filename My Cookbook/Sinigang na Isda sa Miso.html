Sinigang na Isda sa Miso



Sinigang na Isda sa Miso is a traditional Filipino recipe made with fish cooked in a sour and savory broth of tamarind and miso paste. It has been a part of Filipino cuisine for centuries, originating from the pre-colonial era. The addition of miso paste, influenced by Japanese cuisine, has enhanced the flavor of the recipe, making it a popular comfort food enjoyed during gatherings and special occasions in the Philippines.


Ingredients:

  • 2 tbsp Vegetable Oil
  • 4 cloves Garlic,
  • 2 pcs Ginger
  • 1pc Onion
  • 2pcs Tomato
  • 1 pc Finger Chili
  • 0.5 cup Miso
  • 5 cups Water
  • 1 package MAGGI® Magic Sinigang Original Sampalok Mix
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 0.5 kg Maya-Maya 1
  • bunch mustasa

Instructions:

  1. Sauté garlic, ginger, onion, tomato, finger chili and miso in oil. Pour water and simmer.
  2. Pour MAGGI Magic Sinigang® Original Sampalok Mix , fish sauce and sugar.
  3. Add maya-maya and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Stir in mustasa. Transfer into a serving bowl and serve immediately.
